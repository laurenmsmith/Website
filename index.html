Lauren Smith

I am a PhD candidate at Western University in London, ON Canada who is interested in hyperpolarized non-proton metabolic imaging using MRI. I am interested in applying novel imaging methods in pregnancy to learn more about fetoplacental development. I am to continue a career in clinical medical physics, focusing on radiation therapy, and continue to be involved in diagnostic imaging research. I am self-motivated, independent, friendly, and ready to learn.
